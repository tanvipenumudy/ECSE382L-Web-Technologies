About Us


Bennett University is a private interdisciplinary research university located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh in the National Capital Region (NCR), India. Founded in 2016 by Times of India Group the university has a fully residential 68-acre campus.

Bennett University has tied up with Ivy league International institutions for curriculum design, guest lectures by visiting faculty, immersion programs, and other academic support- Georgia Institute of Technology, U.S., Cornell University Law School, U.S., Johnson Cornell University, U.S., Babson Global, U.S., edX, U.S.

The University promotes high quality research focused on finding sustainable solutions to problems of the modern world.

The University hosts four schools, namely School of Engineering and applied sciences, School of Law, School of Management and Times School of Media, currently offering 6 Graduate programmes in Bachelors of Technology, Bachelors in Business administration, BBA-LLB (Hons), BA-LLB (Hons), BA- Journalism & Mass communication in Hindi and English, and 7 post-Graduate Programmes in LLB (Hons), Masters of Business administration, Ph.D. and Post graduate diploma in Digital Journalism/ Business journalism/ Advertising and marketing.
